response to frank chimero - the web's grain reading

concept: a site that starts off w/ a seemingly simple, boxed, image/object that can be dragged around and modified by a someone using their cursor. this would lead to various distortions and continually shift, never returning to its original form until the page is refreshed.

this reading made me think of how templated the web currently is... i know i fall into the habit of creating these boxes and 'templates' myself before i even get the chance to determine what i am putting into my sites

really enjoy the concept of edgelessness and not even thinking outside the box but just kinda.... removing the box out of the equation entirely? its definitely a good way of framing it and i definitely want to try incorporating this line of thinking moving forward
